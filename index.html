ABC, Phonics, Uppercase and Lowercase

Tap a letter. It will say the letter, sound, uppercase, lowercase, and a word.

Numbers 1 to 100

Tap a number to hear it and count. Practice 1–20 first, then 10s to 100.

Simple Addition and Subtraction

Count the apples. Add means more. Subtract means take away.
